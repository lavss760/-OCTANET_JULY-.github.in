APPLE AIRPODS PRO TRUE WIRELESS STEREO (TWS) EARPHONES

Active Noise Cancellation (ANC)

Uses outward- and inward-facing microphones to create superior noise cancellation.

Transparency Mode

Allows you to hear and interact with the world around you by letting outside sound in.

Customizable Fit

Includes three sizes of soft, silicone tips for a comfortable and secure fit.

Adaptive EQ

Automatically tunes music to the shape of your ear for a rich, consistent listening experience.

Battery Life

Offers up to 4.5 hours of listening time on a single charge with ANC enabled, and more than 24 hours with the wireless charging case.

H1 Chip

Provides a more stable wireless connection, lower latency, and efficient battery usage.

Water and Sweat Resistance

Rated IPX4, making them resistant to sweat and water splashes.

Force Sensor Controls

Enables easy control of music playback, answering calls, and switching between ANC and Transparency mode with a pinch.
